About Me

I am currently 22 years old, finishing my degree at Stellenbosch University.

I am studying BSc Mathematical Sciences, majoring in both Computer Science and Genetics .

I know that these are two very diverse and different fields, but that is also how I would describe my personality. I am equally “left” and “right-brained”. I reason logically and think analytically but intuitively and creatively. I am an obsessive planner, focused on details and require everything to match.

That is why I want to pursue a career in which I not only can code the logic but make it look aesthetically pleasing as well.

We are all different. In genetics I learnt that each human being’s blueprint contain different combinations of base pairs, leading to different thought patterns, motives and preferences. One program can be coded in a million different ways as no one of us thinks exactly the same. I want to use my differences to impact not only the computer science industry, but the way people perceive it.
