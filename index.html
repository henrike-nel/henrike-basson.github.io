About Me

I am currently 25 years of age, spending most of my days as a Software Engineer at Bash.

I graduated from Stellenbosch University in 2020 with a BSc Mathematical Sciences degree where I majored in both Computer Science and Genetics

I know that these are two very diverse and different fields, but that is also how I would describe my personality. I am equally “left” and “right-brained”. I reason logically and think analytically but intuitively and creatively. I am an obsessive planner, detail-oriented and passionate about problem-solving and design.

That is why I am currently employed as a full-stack software engineer. Full-stack allows me to write clean code for the front and back-end of software as well as be involved in the designing of user interfaces.

I thus get to quench my desire for aesthetically pleasing designs in addition to working with logic and databases.
